Protect Yourself From Job Scams

While legitimate work-from-home opportunities exist, scammers often target job seekers in Ghana. Please be aware:

  • We will NEVER ask for your Ghana Card PIN or password
  • We will NEVER request payment for training or "starter kits"
  • We will NEVER contact you first via WhatsApp offering jobs
  • Legitimate companies won't ask you to pay fees to receive payment

Always verify company information before sharing personal details. Report suspicious job offers to the Cyber Security Authority.
